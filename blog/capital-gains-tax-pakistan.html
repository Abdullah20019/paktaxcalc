Complete guide to capital gains tax (CGT) in Pakistan for 2026. Learn tax rates on property sales, stock market investments, and how to calculate your CGT liability for real estate and shares.

üí° What is Capital Gains Tax (CGT)?

Capital Gains Tax (CGT) is a tax on the profit you make when you sell an asset for more than you paid for it. In Pakistan, CGT applies to:

  • üè† Real estate (land, houses, apartments, plots)
  • üìà Shares and stocks (listed on Pakistan Stock Exchange)
  • üíº Business assets and equipment
  • üé® Collectibles and valuables (art, jewelry)

üìå Key Point:

CGT is only on PROFIT, not the total sale amount. If you bought property for Rs. 50 lakh and sold for Rs. 80 lakh, you only pay tax on Rs. 30 lakh profit, not Rs. 80 lakh.

üè† Capital Gains Tax on Real Estate (Property)

CGT Rates Based on Holding Period:

Holding Period Tax Rate on Gain Filer Rate Non-Filer Rate
Less than 1 year 15% 15% 30%
1 year to 2 years 12.5% 12.5% 25%
2 years to 3 years 10% 10% 20%
3 years to 4 years 7.5% 7.5% 15%
4 years to 5 years 5% 5% 10%
More than 5 years 0% (Tax-Free!) 0% 0%

‚úÖ Tax-Free After 5 Years!

Hold property for more than 5 years and pay ZERO capital gains tax regardless of profit amount. This is the #1 tax saving strategy for real estate investors!

Example 1: Short-term Sale (2.5 Years)

üí° Stock Market Tax Tip:

Hold shares for more than 12 months to pay ZERO CGT on profits. Day traders and short-term investors pay 15% tax on gains.

üßÆ Stock Market CGT Calculation Example:

Example: Stock Trading (8 Months)

  • Purchased: 1,000 shares @ Rs. 100 = Rs. 1,00,000
  • Sold: 1,000 shares @ Rs. 150 = Rs. 1,50,000
  • Capital Gain: Rs. 50,000
  • Holding Period: 8 months (short-term)
  • CGT Rate: 15%
  • Tax Payable: Rs. 7,500

Note: Your broker will automatically deduct this tax and deposit to FBR.

üíé CGT on Other Assets

1. Mutual Funds & Unit Trusts:

  • ‚úÖ Equity Funds: 12.5% CGT (if redeemed within 1 year)
  • ‚úÖ Debt/Money Market Funds: 10% CGT (short-term)
  • ‚úÖ Long-term (1+ year): Tax-free

2. Gold & Precious Metals:

  • üí∞ Personal jewelry: Usually not tracked for CGT
  • üí∞ Investment gold: 10-15% CGT on profit
  • üí∞ Must declare if profit exceeds Rs. 500,000

3. Cryptocurrency (Legal Gray Area):

  • ‚ö†Ô∏è No specific CGT law yet in Pakistan
  • ‚ö†Ô∏è If converted to PKR: Treat as capital gain (15% recommended)
  • ‚ö†Ô∏è Declare in tax return under "Other Income"

üìã How to Pay Capital Gains Tax

For Property Sales:

  1. At Time of Sale:
    • 1% WHT is deducted by buyer/registrar
    • This is advance tax, not final tax
  2. In Annual Tax Return:
    • Calculate actual CGT based on holding period
    • Declare capital gain in tax return
    • Pay remaining tax or claim refund
  3. File Within: Same tax year of sale

For Stock Market:

  • ‚úÖ Broker automatically deducts CGT
  • ‚úÖ You receive net amount after tax
  • ‚úÖ Still declare in annual tax return

üí° Tax Saving Strategies

1. Hold Assets Long-Term

Property: 5+ years = 0% tax
Stocks: 1+ year = 0% tax

2. Offset Gains with Losses

  • If you have capital loss, offset against capital gain
  • Example: Rs. 100,000 profit on stock A, Rs. 40,000 loss on stock B
  • Net taxable gain: Rs. 60,000 (not Rs. 100,000)

3. Become an Active Filer

  • Filers pay 50% less CGT than non-filers on short-term sales
  • File tax return every year to maintain filer status

4. Gift Property to Spouse/Children

  • Gifting to immediate family: No CGT
  • But they inherit your purchase date (for future CGT calculation)
  • Use only if recipient won't sell soon

5. Reinvest in Another Property (No Direct Exemption)

  • ‚ö†Ô∏è Unlike some countries, Pakistan has NO rollover relief
  • You must pay CGT even if reinvesting profit
  • But can spread tax payment over installments if approved by FBR

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

DON'T:

  • ‚ùå Hide property sale to avoid CGT (FBR tracks property transfers)
  • ‚ùå Undervalue property in documents (FBR uses DC rates)
  • ‚ùå Forget to declare stock market gains in tax return
  • ‚ùå Sell property just before 5-year mark (wait few more months for 0% tax)
  • ‚ùå Mix personal residence sale with investment property (different rules)

üè° Special Case: Primary Residence Exemption

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Note:

Pakistan does NOT have a primary residence exemption like some countries. Even if you sell your own house, CGT applies based on holding period. Only exemption is 5+ year holding rule.
