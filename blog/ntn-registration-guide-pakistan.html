üîç What is NTN (National Tax Number)?

NTN (National Tax Number) is a unique identification number issued by the Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) to every taxpayer in Pakistan. It's mandatory for:

  • ‚úÖ Filing income tax returns
  • ‚úÖ Opening business bank accounts
  • ‚úÖ Registering a company
  • ‚úÖ Freelancers receiving foreign payments
  • ‚úÖ Property transactions above Rs. 4 million
  • ‚úÖ Vehicle registration (above certain engine capacity)
  • ‚úÖ Government contracts and tenders

üí° Quick Fact:

For individuals, your NTN is the same as your CNIC number. You just need to register it with FBR to activate it for tax purposes.

üöÄ How to Get NTN Online - Step by Step

Step 1: Visit FBR IRIS Portal

  1. Go to official FBR website: iris.fbr.gov.pk
  2. Click on "Registration" in the top menu
  3. Select "Register as Taxpayer"

‚ö†Ô∏è Important:

Use Google Chrome or Firefox browser. Internet Explorer often causes errors. Clear your browser cache before starting.

Step 2: Fill Registration Form

Personal Information Section:

  • CNIC Number: Enter your 13-digit CNIC without dashes
  • Full Name: Exactly as written on CNIC
  • Father's/Husband's Name: As per CNIC
  • Date of Birth: Match with CNIC
  • Gender: Select Male/Female/Other

Contact Information:

  • Mobile Number: Active number (you'll get OTP)
  • Email Address: Working email (credentials sent here)
  • Residential Address: Current address with city/postal code

Taxpayer Type:

  • Select "Individual" for personal registration
  • Select "Association of Persons (AOP)" for partnerships
  • Select "Company" for registered businesses

Step 3: Verify Your Mobile & Email

  1. Enter the OTP (One-Time Password) sent to your mobile
  2. Click "Verify Mobile"
  3. Check your email for verification code
  4. Enter email verification code
  5. Click "Verify Email"

üö® Troubleshooting:

  • ‚ùå OTP not received? Check if number is correct, wait 2 minutes, or click "Resend OTP"
  • ‚ùå Email not received? Check spam/junk folder
  • ‚ùå Already registered error? You may already have NTN. Try password recovery

Step 4: Set Password

  • Create a strong password (min 8 characters)
  • Must include: uppercase, lowercase, number, special character
  • Example: MyPass@123
  • Confirm password by entering again
  • Save this password safely!

Step 5: Submit & Get NTN Certificate

  1. Review all information carefully
  2. Click "Submit"
  3. Your NTN will be generated instantly
  4. NTN certificate will appear on screen (for individuals: NTN = CNIC)
  5. Download and save PDF certificate
  6. Check your email for confirmation and login credentials

üñ®Ô∏è How to Download NTN Certificate

Method 1: During Registration

  • After successful registration, certificate appears automatically
  • Click "Download Certificate" button
  • Save PDF to your computer

Method 2: Login to IRIS Portal

  1. Go to iris.fbr.gov.pk
  2. Click "Login"
  3. Enter your NTN/CNIC and password
  4. Go to "Registration" ‚Üí "View Registration"
  5. Click "Print/Download Certificate"

Method 3: Get from FBR Office

  • Visit nearest FBR Regional Tax Office (RTO)
  • Bring original CNIC
  • Request NTN certificate print
  • Usually issued same day (free of cost)

‚úÖ How to Verify Your NTN is Active

Online Verification:

  1. Visit FBR Verification Portal
  2. Select "NTN Verification"
  3. Enter your NTN/CNIC number
  4. Click "Verify"
  5. Your registration status will appear

Via SMS:

  • Send SMS: NTN [Your CNIC]
  • To: 9966
  • You'll receive NTN status instantly

üí∞ NTN Registration Fee

‚úÖ COMPLETELY FREE!

NTN registration is 100% free in Pakistan. FBR does not charge any fee. Beware of agents charging money - you don't need them!

üîê After Getting NTN - What's Next?

1. Activate Income Tax Registration

  • Login to IRIS portal
  • Go to "Registration" ‚Üí "Tax Registration"
  • Select "Income Tax"
  • Submit (approval within 24-48 hours)

2. Link Your Bank Account

  • Provide NTN to your bank
  • Bank will link it to your account
  • Required for tax deduction reporting

3. Update FBR Profile

  • Add business details if applicable
  • Update contact information
  • Add alternate email/phone
