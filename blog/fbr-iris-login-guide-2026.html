FBR IRIS Login Guide 2026 - IRIS FBR Login, Reset Password & Common Errors

PakTaxCalc Team • March 11, 2026 • 14 min read

If you are trying to access the tax portal, the first thing you need is a smooth FBR IRIS login. Many users search for the same task using phrases like IRIS FBR login or IRIS login FBR. In theory, the process is simple. In practice, many users get stuck on passwords, captcha errors, old accounts, browser issues, or recovery steps that do not seem clear the first time.

This guide explains the full IRIS login Pakistan process in plain language. You will learn how to sign in, what to do if you forgot your password, how recovery usually works, and how to fix the most common problems without wasting time.

If you need the broader portal overview, registration flow, filing steps, notices, and certificates, read our main FBR IRIS portal guide. This article focuses specifically on login, recovery, and access issues.

Official login page

The official IRIS portal is available at iris.fbr.gov.pk. Always check the domain before entering your CNIC, NTN, password, or OTP.

What Is FBR IRIS Login?

FBR IRIS login is the sign-in process for Pakistan's official income tax portal. After logging in, you can use IRIS for tasks such as:

  • filing income tax returns
  • submitting a wealth statement
  • checking notices and replies
  • viewing registration details
  • downloading acknowledgments or certificates
  • creating payment references in connected workflows

In most individual cases, you sign in with your registration number, which is commonly linked to your CNIC, along with your password and the captcha shown on the page.

What You Need Before You Login to IRIS

Before you try to log in, keep these things ready:

  • Your registration number, usually CNIC or NTN
  • Your IRIS account password
  • Access to your registered mobile number or email in case recovery is needed
  • A stable internet connection
  • A supported browser such as Chrome or Firefox

Important reminder

If you are already registered, do not try to create a fresh account just because you forgot the password. In that situation, the better route is usually account recovery through the forgot password option.

How to Login to FBR IRIS Step by Step

The normal login flow is straightforward:

  1. Open iris.fbr.gov.pk.
  2. Click the Login option if needed.
  3. Enter your Registration No. such as CNIC or NTN.
  4. Enter your password carefully.
  5. Type the captcha exactly as shown.
  6. Click Login to access the dashboard.

If the credentials are correct, you should reach your IRIS dashboard. If the page reloads or blocks access, the problem is usually one of the common issues explained below rather than the portal being permanently down.

How to Reset Forgotten FBR IRIS Password

The most common support issue with FBR IRIS password reset is that people do not remember which contact details are linked with the account. Still, the recovery flow is usually simple if your mobile number and email are active.

  1. Go to the IRIS login page.
  2. Click Forgot Password.
  3. Enter your registration number.
  4. Follow the verification flow shown by the portal.
  5. Use the code or verification process sent to your registered contact details.
  6. Create a new password and log in again.

If you no longer have access to the registered email or mobile number, recovery may become more difficult. In that case, you may need to update your profile or contact FBR support through the proper channel.

How to Create a Strong IRIS Password

Many login problems start after people change a password quickly and then forget the exact combination. A better approach is to create a secure password that is still easy for you to store safely.

  • Use uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Add numbers and a special character
  • Avoid obvious passwords based on your name or CNIC
  • Do not reuse the same password from email or banking apps
  • Save it in a trusted password manager or secure note

Most Common FBR IRIS Login Errors

Most login issues fall into a small number of patterns. Once you identify which one you are facing, the fix becomes easier.

1. Wrong password

This is the most obvious issue, but it still causes the highest number of failed attempts. Check uppercase letters, special characters, and whether your keyboard is switching language or capitalization.

2. Captcha not working

If the captcha does not appear properly or keeps rejecting your input, refresh the page first. If the issue continues, clear browser cache, disable blockers that may interfere, and try another browser.

3. Session timeout

IRIS can time out during inactivity. If you wait too long on one screen, the session may expire and force a new login. This is normal behavior on many government portals.

4. Already registered account

Some users try to sign up again after forgetting their credentials. If IRIS says you are already registered, that usually means the correct path is password recovery, not a fresh registration.

5. Browser or cache problem

Government portals sometimes behave badly with old cached files or unsupported browser settings. A private window, fresh browser session, or switching from one browser to another often solves this quickly.

What If You No Longer Have Access to Your Registered Email or Mobile?

This is one of the harder account recovery situations. If your old email is closed or your mobile number has changed, the standard recovery process may not work smoothly because the verification step depends on the contact details already linked with your account.

In this case, the practical steps are:

  • first confirm whether you still remember the registration number correctly
  • try the forgot password flow once using the official portal
  • check whether any recovery prompt allows access through the alternate registered contact
  • if that fails, use official FBR support channels instead of repeated guesswork

Repeated failed attempts usually do not solve this problem. The real solution is to restore access through the proper registered details or update the account through the official channel where required.

First-Time User Checklist Before IRIS Login

If this is your first time using the portal, a small checklist can save a lot of confusion. Many people think the portal is broken when the real issue is simply missing registration details or an old email address.

  • Confirm whether you are already registered before trying to sign up again.
  • Keep your CNIC or NTN ready exactly as entered in the system.
  • Use a stable browser session without too many extensions running.
  • Keep your registered mobile and email accessible before starting recovery.
  • Plan to save your updated password immediately after reset.

This sounds basic, but it is the difference between a five-minute login and an hour of retrying the wrong path.

IRIS Registration, Login, and Password Reset: What Is the Difference?

Users often mix up these three actions, which creates unnecessary problems. They look related, but they solve different issues.

Action When to use it When not to use it
RegistrationYou have never created an IRIS account beforeYou already have an account but forgot the password
LoginYou know your registration number and passwordYou no longer remember the password
Password resetYou already have an account but cannot access itYou have never registered at all

If IRIS says you are already registered, that is a strong sign that password recovery is the right step. Your next move should not be another registration attempt.

Common Mistakes That Make IRIS Login Harder

Some login problems are technical, but many are caused by user behavior. These are the most common avoidable mistakes:

  • trying to register a second time instead of recovering the old account
  • using a mobile browser full of saved autofill data that inserts the wrong password
  • not checking whether the registered mobile number is still active
  • copy-pasting passwords with an extra hidden space at the start or end
  • opening multiple IRIS tabs and getting logged out in the middle of a session
  • keeping ad blockers, VPNs, or privacy tools active when captcha is already unstable

These issues are simple, but they waste a lot of time because users often assume the problem is entirely on FBR's side.

Safety Tips Before Entering Your IRIS Password

  • Always confirm that you are on the official `iris.fbr.gov.pk` page.
  • Do not share your password or OTP with a third party.
  • Avoid logging in from public computers if possible.
  • If you must use a shared computer, log out fully after use.
  • Save important PDFs and acknowledgments after logging in successfully.

A lot of users focus only on getting access, but security matters too. Your IRIS account connects to sensitive tax data, so basic login hygiene is worth taking seriously.

When to Contact FBR Support

Not every problem should be handled by repeated retries. Sometimes the right move is to stop, document the issue, and contact official support.

That is usually the better option if:

  • your registered mobile and email are both outdated
  • the system shows you are already registered but recovery is failing
  • the portal accepts the reset flow but still blocks access afterward
  • your profile information appears mismatched with your actual registration record
  • you need formal help with a linked tax profile issue, not just a password problem

When contacting support, keep your registration number, screenshots if possible, and a clear summary of the problem ready. That usually makes resolution faster.

Best Practices for Smoother IRIS Login Sessions

A smooth login is not only about the first sign-in screen. It is also about how you use the portal after access is granted. These small habits reduce repeat login trouble:

  • log out properly after finishing your work instead of just closing the browser tab
  • avoid opening multiple IRIS windows at the same time
  • save drafts frequently when working on long forms
  • do important filing work on a stable internet connection
  • keep your registered mobile number and email updated whenever possible

These habits matter because many users think login is the only issue, when the real frustration starts from expired sessions or half-saved work after logging in successfully.

Common Myths About FBR IRIS Login

Myth 1: If login fails, I need a new account

Usually false. If you are already registered, the better first step is recovery, not re-registration.

Myth 2: Mobile login problems mean the account is blocked

Not necessarily. Sometimes the issue is just browser compatibility, captcha display, or session handling on the phone.

Myth 3: Password reset always works instantly

Recovery depends on your registered contact details and the current portal flow. If your old email or mobile number is no longer available, the process may need extra support steps.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I login to FBR IRIS?

Open the official portal, enter your registration number, password, captcha, and then click login.

What should I do if I forgot my FBR IRIS password?

Use the forgot password option on the login page and complete the verification flow using your registered contact details.

Can I login to IRIS with CNIC?

Yes. Individual taxpayers commonly use their CNIC-based registration number to access IRIS.

Why is IRIS rejecting my login?

The usual reasons are wrong password, captcha failure, browser cache problems, session timeout, or trying to access an already registered account with the wrong credentials.

How can I fix captcha issues?

Refresh the page, clear cache, disable interfering browser extensions, and try Chrome or Firefox.

Can I recover an already registered account?

Yes. If the portal says the account already exists, the normal solution is account recovery through forgot password rather than a new registration.

Is mobile login okay for IRIS?

For simple login, yes. For full return filing or wealth statement work, desktop use is usually easier and more reliable.

Where can I get the full IRIS portal overview?

A smooth FBR IRIS login usually comes down to a few basics: correct registration number, correct password, working recovery details, and a clean browser session.

If login fails, do not rush into creating a new account or guessing repeatedly. Most problems are solved by password recovery, browser cleanup, or using the correct registered details.

Once you are inside the portal, use the right guide for the next step, whether that is filing a return, checking your profile, or preparing your wealth statement.
