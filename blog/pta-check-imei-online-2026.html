PTA Check Complete Guide 2026: PTA IMEI Check, PTA Approved Check & PTA Tax Check

PakTaxCalc Team � February 21, 2026

Every mobile phone in Pakistan needs PTA check approval to work on local networks. Whether you're buying a used phone, traveling abroad, or just want to verify your device status, knowing how to do PTA IMEI check online, PTA approved check, and PTA tax check is essential in 2026. This complete guide covers all methods with step-by-step instructions.

PTA's DIRBS system blocks unregistered phones after 60 days. A simple SMS to 8484 or online check at dirbs.pta.gov.pk tells you if your phone is compliant, needs tax payment, or is already blocked. Most people prefer the SMS method because it's instant and works everywhere without internet.

Method 1: PTA IMEI Check via SMS 8484 (Most Popular)

This is the fastest way to do PTA check IMEI. No internet, no app, just works everywhere in Pakistan:

  1. Dial *#06# on your phone to get 15-digit IMEI number
  2. Open Messages app and type your IMEI number
  3. Send to 8484 (costs Rs. 2 + tax)
  4. Get instant reply in 10 seconds with status

Dual SIM phones? Send BOTH IMEI numbers separately to 8484. The reply shows exact status for each SIM slot.

What the SMS Reply Means:

  • ? "Compliant" = PTA approved, phone works normally
  • ?? "Non-compliant" = PTA tax due, 60 days to pay
  • ? "Blocked" = No SIM cards work, needs registration

Method 2: PTA Approved Check Online (DIRBS Portal)

For detailed PTA approved check online, use the official DIRBS website:

  1. Visit dirbs.pta.gov.pk
  2. Enter your 15-digit IMEI number
  3. Complete CAPTCHA verification
  4. Click "Check Status" for complete details

This method shows registration date, tax payment status, expiry date, and device model validation. Perfect for PTA check online when you need full history.

How to Check PTA Approved Before Buying Used Phone

70% of used phones have PTA tax pending! Always do how to check PTA approved before paying:

  • Step 1: Ask seller for IMEI (*#06#)
  • Step 2: Send IMEI to 8484 yourself
  • Step 3: If "Non-compliant" = seller must pay tax first
  • Step 4: Dual SIM? Check both IMEIs

Never buy "Non-compliant" phones without tax receipt! You'll lose access after 60 days.

PTA Mobile Registration Online Check � 4 Methods

Method 1: USSD Registration (*8484#)

Dial *8484# ? Select "Register Foreign Device" ? Enter CNIC + IMEI ? Pay tax

Method 2: DIRBS Web Portal

dirbs.pta.gov.pk ? Login ? New Application ? Pay via EasyPaisa/JazzCash/Bank

Method 3: PTA Mobile App

Download "PTA DIRBS" from Google Play ? Scan IMEI ? Pay ? Track status

PTA SIM Check � New 2026 Rule

PTA now pairs SIM cards with IMEIs. If phone IMEI is blocked, no SIM works on any network. Always do PTA SIM check by inserting SIM and sending IMEI to 8484.

Common PTA Check Problems & Solutions

  • "Invalid IMEI": Check *#06# has exactly 15 digits
  • "Dual IMEI mismatch": Both IMEIs must belong to same phone model
  • "Tax already paid": Call PTA helpline 0800-55055
  • "Blocked after 60 days": Pay PTA tax immediately via EasyPaisa
