10 Smart Tips to Reduce Your Salary Tax in Pakistan

PakTaxCalc Team ‚Ä¢ February 15, 2026

Salary tax can take a significant chunk out of your monthly income. Here are 10 proven strategies to legally reduce your tax burden and increase your take-home pay.

1. Maximize Provident Fund Contributions

Your employer's contribution to your provident fund is tax-free. Consider increasing your contribution percentage to reduce taxable income while building retirement savings.

2. Claim Medical Allowance Properly

Medical allowance up to 10% of basic salary or Rs. 120,000 per year is tax-free. Ensure your employer structures this allowance correctly in your salary package.

3. Utilize Transport Allowance

Transport allowance can be tax-free if properly documented. Keep records of commuting expenses to justify this deduction.
