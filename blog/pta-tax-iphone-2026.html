What is PTA Tax on iPhone?

PTA tax on iPhone is a regulatory fee charged by the Pakistan Telecommunication Authority on all imported mobile devices, including iPhones. It is collected through the DIRBS (Device Identification, Registration & Blocking System) and must be paid before or shortly after arriving in Pakistan with an unregistered phone.

Without this registration, your iPhone will be blocked by all Pakistani mobile networks � Jazz, Zong, Telenor, and Ufone � after a grace period. Once blocked, no SIM card will work until the tax is paid and the device is approved.

Why is PTA Tax Charged in Pakistan?

The iPhone registration tax Pakistan was introduced to:

  • Discourage illegal import and smuggling of mobile devices
  • Generate customs and regulatory revenue for the Pakistani government via FBR
  • Ensure all devices meet local telecommunications standards
  • Protect official importers and authorised dealers
  • Create a national registry of mobile devices for security purposes

The tax is calculated based on the customs value of the phone as declared by FBR. Higher-end models like iPhone 16 Pro Max carry a significantly higher PTA tax on iPhone than budget or older models.

How to Pay PTA Tax Online (DIRBS Process)

Paying your iPhone tax in Pakistan online is straightforward. Here's the step-by-step process:

  1. Visit dirbs.pta.gov.pk or dial *8484#
  2. Select "New Device Registration"
  3. Enter your IMEI number (use *#06# to find it)
  4. Choose CNIC or Passport method
  5. Enter your CNIC / Passport number
  6. Review the calculated PTA tax amount
  7. Pay via EasyPaisa, JazzCash, or online banking
  8. Receive confirmation SMS � registration active within 24�72 hours

How Long Can You Use iPhone Without PTA Approval?

PTA provides a 60-day grace period for newly imported phones. During this window, your iPhone will work normally on all Pakistani networks. After 60 days without registration, the IMEI is blocked.

  • Travellers arriving from abroad get a 4-month temporary free registration via *8484#
  • After the grace period, all SIM services stop permanently until tax is paid
  • Wi-Fi still works even on a blocked phone � but no SIM access

What Happens If You Don't Pay PTA Tax?

Skipping PTA approved iPhone registration has serious consequences:

  • ? All four major networks (Jazz, Zong, Telenor, Ufone) block your SIM
  • ? You cannot make calls, send SMS, or use mobile data
  • ? No SIM card from any operator will work in the device
  • ? You cannot resell the phone at full market value
  • ? Wi-Fi still works � so apps like WhatsApp on Wi-Fi remain accessible

The only solution after blocking is to pay the full PTA tax on iPhone and register through DIRBS. The unblocking process can take 24�72 hours after payment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is PTA tax on iPhone 16 Pro Max in 2026?

PTA tax on iPhone 16 Pro Max is Rs. 213,631 on CNIC and Rs. 182,710 on Passport as of February 2026. This is the highest in the iPhone 16 lineup.

What is the PTA tax on iPhone 15 in Pakistan?

PTA tax on iPhone 15 is Rs. 134,750 on CNIC and Rs. 111,000 on Passport. After FBR's January 2026 revision for used iPhones, the tax on older units may differ slightly.

Is iPhone PTA tax on Passport cheaper than CNIC?

Yes. iPhone PTA tax via Passport is 15�20% cheaper than CNIC on all models. For example on an iPhone 16 Pro Max, you save over Rs. 30,000 by using the Passport method. It is only available for people who recently travelled abroad.

How do I check PTA tax on my iPhone before buying?

Get the seller's IMEI number by dialling *#06#, then send it via SMS to 8484. The reply confirms if the phone is compliant, non-compliant (tax due), or blocked. Always do this before paying.

Does iPhone storage (128GB vs 256GB) affect PTA tax?

No. PTA tax is the same for all storage variants of the same model. Whether you have a 128GB or 512GB iPhone 16 Pro Max, the tax amount is identical.

Can I pay PTA tax in installments?

No. PTA tax must be paid as a lump sum payment through the DIRBS portal. Payment methods include EasyPaisa, JazzCash, bank transfers, and 1-Link. There is no installment option currently available.

What is the cheapest iPhone to register with PTA?

The iPhone 5, 5C, and 5S have the lowest PTA tax at just Rs. 6,400 (Passport) and Rs. 7,843 (CNIC). Among modern models, the iPhone SE (2020/2022) at around Rs. 38,000�47,000 is the most affordable to register.
