What is PTA Mobile Tax?

PTA mobile tax is a customs duty charged by Pakistan Telecommunication Authority on mobile phones brought from abroad. When you enter Pakistan with a foreign phone, you must register it with PTA and pay the applicable tax within 60 days, or your phone will be blocked from Pakistani networks.

PTA Tax Rates 2026

Tax rates depend on:

  • Phone Value: Higher value = higher tax percentage
  • Registration Type: CNIC (Pakistani) vs Passport (Tourist)
  • Phone Brand: Premium brands may have additional charges

Tax Rate Structure:

How to Register Your Phone with PTA

  1. Check IMEI: Dial *#06# to get your phone's IMEI number
  2. Visit PTA Portal: Go to dirbs.pta.gov.pk
  3. Enter Details: Provide IMEI, passport/CNIC, and entry date
  4. Get Tax Amount: System will calculate tax based on phone value
  5. Pay Online: Use credit card, debit card, or bank transfer
  6. Confirmation: Phone will be unblocked within 24 hours

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not registering within 60 days: Phone will be blocked
  • Using fake/clone IMEI: Phone will be permanently blocked
  • Forgetting to pay full tax: Registration won't complete
  • Wrong phone value: Always check official rates

FAQs

Q: Can I register a phone without visiting Pakistan?

A: No, you must physically bring the phone to Pakistan and register at entry or within 60 days.

Q: What happens if I don't pay PTA tax?

A: After 60 days, your phone will be blocked from all Pakistani cellular networks (Jazz, Zong, Telenor, Ufone).

Q: Can I use my phone without SIM?

A: Yes, Wi-Fi will work, but you cannot use any Pakistani SIM card until registered.
