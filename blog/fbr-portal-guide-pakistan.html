üåê What is FBR IRIS Portal?

IRIS (Integrated Registration & Identification System) is the official online portal of the Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) for all tax-related services in Pakistan. It allows you to:

  • ‚úÖ Register for NTN (National Tax Number)
  • ‚úÖ File income tax returns online
  • ‚úÖ Make tax payments
  • ‚úÖ Download tax certificates and receipts
  • ‚úÖ Check filer/non-filer status
  • ‚úÖ Track refunds
  • ‚úÖ Register for sales tax (if applicable)

üöÄ Getting Started: First-Time Registration

Step 1: Access the Portal

  1. Open Google Chrome or Firefox browser (recommended)
  2. Visit: iris.fbr.gov.pk
  3. Click on "Registration" in the top menu
  4. Select "Register as Taxpayer"

Step 2: Fill Registration Form

Required Information:

  • üìå CNIC Number: 13 digits without dashes (e.g., 3520212345678)
  • üìå Full Name: Exactly as on CNIC
  • üìå Father's Name: As per CNIC
  • üìå Date of Birth: Match with CNIC
  • üìå Mobile Number: Active (for OTP verification)
  • üìå Email Address: Working email
  • üìå Residential Address: Current complete address

Step 3: Verify Mobile & Email

  • Enter OTP sent to your mobile
  • Enter verification code sent to email
  • If OTP not received: Wait 2 minutes, check spam folder, or click "Resend"

Step 4: Create Password

  • Minimum 8 characters
  • Must include: uppercase, lowercase, number, special character
  • Example: MyTax@2026
  • Save this password securely!

Step 5: Submit & Get NTN

  • Review all information carefully
  • Click "Submit"
  • Your NTN certificate will be generated instantly
  • Download and save PDF certificate

‚úÖ Registration Complete!

For individuals, your NTN is the same as your CNIC number. You can now login and access all FBR services.

üîê How to Login to IRIS Portal

Login Steps:

  1. Go to iris.fbr.gov.pk
  2. Click "Login" button (top right)
  3. Enter your NTN/CNIC (without dashes)
  4. Enter your password
  5. Solve the CAPTCHA (enter letters/numbers shown)
  6. Click "Login"

Forgot Password?

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
  2. Enter your NTN/CNIC
  3. Select recovery method: Email or SMS
  4. Enter verification code received
  5. Create new password

üìã How to File Income Tax Return on IRIS

Step 1: Login & Navigate

  1. Login to IRIS portal
  2. Go to "Returns" menu
  3. Click "Income Tax Return"
  4. Select tax year (e.g., 2026 for income earned in 2025)

Step 2: Choose Return Form

Your Situation Form to Use
Salaried person (income below Rs. 10M) Wealth Statement
Business owner / Self-employed ITR Form (Auto-selected)
Multiple income sources ITR Form
Company/AOP Company Return Form

Step 3: Fill Income Details

For Salaried Persons:

  • Salary Income: Enter gross annual salary
  • Tax Deducted: Enter total tax deducted by employer (from salary slips)
  • Allowances: Enter tax-free allowances (medical, HRA, etc.)
  • Other Income: Bank interest, rental income, etc.

For Business/Freelancers:

  • Gross Receipts: Total income received
  • Business Expenses: Deductible expenses
  • Net Profit: Auto-calculated
  • Advance Tax Paid: If any quarterly payments made

Step 4: Declare Assets & Liabilities

Assets to Declare:

  • üí∞ Bank account balances (as of June 30)
  • üè† Property (land, houses, apartments)
  • üöó Vehicles
  • üíç Jewelry and valuables
  • üìà Investments (stocks, mutual funds)
  • üíµ Cash on hand (if significant amount)

Liabilities to Declare:

  • üè¶ Bank loans
  • üí≥ Credit card debt
  • üë§ Personal loans

Step 5: Attach Documents (if Required)

  • Salary certificate from employer
  • Bank statements
  • Tax deduction certificates
  • Investment proofs
  • Property documents

Step 6: Review & Submit

  1. Click "Preview" to see complete return
  2. Review all entries carefully
  3. If tax payable: Pay online via credit card or generate challan
  4. Click "Submit"
  5. Enter your password to confirm
  6. Download acknowledgment receipt

‚è∞ Important Deadline:

Tax returns must be filed by September 30 every year for the previous year's income. Late filing penalty: Rs. 1,000 - 40,000.

üí∞ How to Make Tax Payments Online

Method 1: Online Payment (Credit/Debit Card)

  1. Login to IRIS portal
  2. Go to "Payments" ‚Üí "Online Payment"
  3. Select payment type (Income Tax, Advance Tax, etc.)
  4. Enter amount to pay
  5. Choose payment method: Credit Card / Debit Card / Bank Account
  6. Complete payment through bank's secure gateway
  7. Download payment receipt

Method 2: Bank Challan (Cash/Cheque)

  1. Login to IRIS portal
  2. Go to "Payments" ‚Üí "Generate Challan"
  3. Select payment type and enter amount
  4. Download and print challan (PSID)
  5. Visit any designated bank branch
  6. Submit challan with cash/cheque
  7. Payment reflects in IRIS within 24-48 hours

üîç How to Check Filer/Non-Filer Status

Method 1: IRIS Portal

  1. Login to IRIS portal
  2. Go to "Inquiry" ‚Üí "Active Taxpayer List"
  3. Your filer status will be displayed

Method 2: FBR Website (No Login Required)

  1. Visit: e.fbr.gov.pk/esbn/
  2. Select "Active Taxpayer List"
  3. Enter your NTN/CNIC
  4. Click "Search"
  5. Status: "Active" = Filer, "Not Found" = Non-Filer

Method 3: SMS

  • Send SMS: ATL [Your CNIC]
  • To: 9966
  • You'll receive filer status instantly

üìÑ How to Download NTN Certificate

  1. Login to IRIS portal
  2. Go to "Registration" ‚Üí "View Registration"
  3. Your registration details will appear
  4. Click "Print/Download Certificate"
  5. PDF will download automatically
  6. Save for future use

üíµ How to Track Tax Refund

  1. Login to IRIS portal
  2. Go to "Payments" ‚Üí "Refund Status"
  3. Select tax year
  4. View refund processing status:
    • "Under Process" - FBR reviewing your claim
    • "Approved" - Refund sanctioned
    • "Paid" - Amount transferred to your bank
    • "Rejected" - Claim denied (check reason)

‚è±Ô∏è Refund Timeline:

Tax refunds typically take 45-90 days to process. Ensure your bank account is linked to IRIS for direct transfer.

üîÑ How to Update Personal Information

  1. Login to IRIS portal
  2. Go to "Registration" ‚Üí "Update Profile"
  3. You can update:
    • Mobile number
    • Email address
    • Residential address
    • Bank account details
  4. Click "Save Changes"
  5. Verify with OTP sent to new mobile/email

‚ö†Ô∏è Cannot Update Online:

Name, CNIC, and Father's Name cannot be changed online. You must visit nearest FBR Regional Tax Office (RTO) with CNIC and written request.
