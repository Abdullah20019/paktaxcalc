Running a business in Pakistan comes with tax responsibilities. This guide covers everything small business owners and freelancers need to know about business taxes in 2026.

Business Tax Rates 2026

Annual Income Tax Rate
Up to Rs. 600,000 0%
Rs. 600,001 - 1,200,000 5%
Rs. 1,200,001 - 2,400,000 15%
Rs. 2,400,001+ 25-35%

Steps to Register Your Business

  1. Get NTN (National Tax Number) - Register at FBR office or online
  2. Register with SECP - If operating as company
  3. Get Sales Tax Registration - If turnover exceeds Rs. 10 million
  4. Open Business Bank Account - Separate from personal

Tax Deductions for Businesses

  • Office rent and utilities
  • Employee salaries
  • Business equipment and supplies
  • Marketing and advertising costs
  • Professional fees (lawyer, accountant)
  • Business travel expenses

Freelancer Tax Guide

Freelancers earning from platforms like Upwork, Fiverr, and international clients must:

  • Register as freelancer with FBR (0.25% tax rate)
  • File quarterly tax returns
  • Keep records of all foreign payments
  • Declare cryptocurrency earnings
