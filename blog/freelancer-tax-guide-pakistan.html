üìä Do Pakistani Freelancers Need to Pay Tax?

Yes, all freelancers earning income in Pakistan must pay tax. Whether you work on Fiverr, Upwork, Freelancer.com, or receive payments through PayPal, Payoneer, or Wise, your income is taxable under Pakistani law.

‚úÖ Good News for Small Freelancers:

If your annual income is less than Rs. 600,000 (Rs. 50,000/month), you pay ZERO income tax! You still need to file a return showing your income.

üìã How to Register as a Freelancer with FBR

Step 1: Get Your NTN (National Tax Number)

  1. Visit FBR IRIS Portal: iris.fbr.gov.pk
  2. Click "Registration" ‚Üí "Register as Taxpayer"
  3. Fill in your CNIC details and personal information
  4. Select "Individual" as taxpayer type
  5. Your NTN will be generated instantly (same as your CNIC)

Step 2: Register for Income Tax

  1. Login to IRIS portal with your NTN and password
  2. Go to "Registration" ‚Üí "Tax Registration"
  3. Select "Income Tax" and submit
  4. You'll receive confirmation within 24 hours

üí≥ Tax on Different Payment Methods

1. PayPal (via Payoneer/Wise)

  • Withholding Tax: 1% deducted by bank when you withdraw
  • Income Tax: You pay based on annual slabs above
  • Total Tax: 1% + income tax rate for your bracket

2. Direct Bank Transfer (Payoneer, Wise, Pioneer)

  • Withholding Tax: 1% on foreign remittances
  • Final Tax: Additional income tax as per slabs

3. Cryptocurrency Payments

  • Legally gray area in Pakistan
  • If converted to PKR through banks: 1% WHT applies
  • Declare as "Foreign Income" in tax return

üìù How to File Freelancer Tax Return

Documents You'll Need:

  • ‚úÖ Bank statements showing foreign remittances
  • ‚úÖ Fiverr/Upwork earnings report (download from platform)
  • ‚úÖ Payment gateway statements (Payoneer, Wise)
  • ‚úÖ CNIC copy
  • ‚úÖ NTN certificate

Filing Process:

  1. Login to FBR IRIS Portal
  2. Go to "Returns" ‚Üí "Income Tax Return"
  3. Select tax year (e.g., 2026 for income earned in 2025)
  4. Choose appropriate form:
    • For simple income: Use "Wealth Statement" option
    • For detailed filing: Use ITR Form (based on income level)
  5. Enter your freelance income under "Business Income" or "Income from Other Sources"
  6. Attach withholding tax certificates from banks
  7. Submit and download acknowledgment receipt
