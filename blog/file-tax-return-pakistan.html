Filing income tax returns in Pakistan might seem complicated, but with this step-by-step guide, you'll complete your tax filing easily and avoid penalties. Whether you're a salaried individual, business owner, or freelancer, this guide covers everything you need to know.

Tax return filing deadline for Tax Year 2026 is September 30, 2026. Late filing attracts penalties!

Who Must File Tax Returns?

You must file tax returns if you:

  • Earn more than Rs. 600,000 per year (Rs. 50,000/month)
  • Own a property or vehicle
  • Have a business or professional income
  • Want to open a bank account or travel abroad
  • Are already registered as a taxpayer (NTN holder)

üí° Pro Tip:

Even if your income is below the taxable limit, filing a return keeps you in the Active Taxpayers List (ATL), which gives you benefits like lower withholding tax rates!

Step-by-Step Filing Process

Step 1: Register on IRIS Portal

  1. Visit: iris.fbr.gov.pk
  2. Click "Register" if you don't have an account
  3. Enter your CNIC and mobile number
  4. Verify OTP sent to your mobile
  5. Create a password

Step 2: Gather Required Documents

  • Salary Individuals: Salary slips, tax deduction certificates
  • Business Owners: Profit & loss statements, balance sheet
  • Freelancers: Bank statements, PayPal/Wise transactions
  • Property Owners: Property documents, rental income proof
  • Everyone: Bank statements, CNIC, NTN certificate

Step 3: Login and Start Filing

  1. Login to IRIS portal
  2. Click "Income Tax Return"
  3. Select "Create New Return"
  4. Choose tax year: 2026
  5. Select return type: Salaried/Business/Both

Step 4: Fill Income Details

For Salaried Individuals:

  • ‚Ä¢ Enter employer details (NTN, name)
  • ‚Ä¢ Total salary received
  • ‚Ä¢ Tax already deducted
  • ‚Ä¢ Allowances and benefits

Step 5: Declare Assets & Liabilities

You must declare:

  • Assets: Properties, vehicles, bank accounts, investments
  • Liabilities: Loans, mortgages, credit cards
  • Living Expenses: Monthly household expenses

Step 6: Calculate Tax

IRIS portal will automatically calculate your tax based on entered data. Review carefully!

Step 7: Submit & Get Acknowledgment

  1. Review all information
  2. Click "Submit"
  3. Download acknowledgment receipt (PDF)
  4. Save acknowledgment number
  5. Print and keep for records
