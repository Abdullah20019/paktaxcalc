✅ How to Verify NTN Number in Pakistan – Complete Guide 2026

PakTaxCalc Team • March 5, 2026 • 10 min read

Wondering how to verify NTN number online in Pakistan? Whether you need to check your own NTN status, verify a business partner's tax registration, or confirm your active taxpayer status before a property transaction, this comprehensive guide covers all methods of NTN verification in Pakistan. Learn how to use the FBR portal, SMS service, and helpline to check NTN numbers quickly and accurately.

🔍 What is NTN and Why Verification Matters

Understanding NTN (National Tax Number)

The National Tax Number (NTN) is a unique 13-digit identification number issued by Pakistan's Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) to every registered taxpayer. For individual taxpayers, the NTN is typically the same as their CNIC number, making it easy to remember and use for tax-related purposes.

Your NTN serves as your official identity in Pakistan's tax system and is required for various financial and legal transactions. Without a valid, active NTN, you may face higher withholding taxes, difficulties in banking transactions, and complications in property and vehicle dealings.

Why You Need to Verify NTN

NTN verification is essential for several important reasons:

  • ✅ Confirm Active Status: Ensure your NTN is active and you're on the Active Taxpayers List (ATL)
  • ✅ Avoid Higher Taxes: Inactive NTN holders pay higher withholding taxes on banking transactions
  • ✅ Business Verification: Verify tax status of vendors, suppliers, and business partners
  • ✅ Property Transactions: Required when buying or selling property in Pakistan
  • ✅ Vehicle Registration: Essential for registering or transferring vehicles
  • ✅ Bank Account Opening: Most banks require active NTN for account opening
  • ✅ Tender Participation: Government contracts require verified NTN status

💡 Pro Tip

Always verify your NTN status before important transactions. An inactive NTN can result in double withholding tax (up to 100% more) on cash withdrawals, banking transactions, and property dealings.

🌐 Step-by-Step Guide: Verify NTN Online via FBR

The FBR e-Services portal is the most reliable way to verify NTN number online. Follow these detailed steps to check your NTN status:

Step 1: Access the FBR e-Services Portal

  1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Edge recommended)
  2. Navigate to the official FBR e-Services portal: e.fbr.gov.pk/esbn/
  3. Wait for the page to fully load – the portal displays various verification options

⚠️ Important Security Note

Always verify you're on the official FBR website (e.fbr.gov.pk). Look for the secure HTTPS connection and official FBR branding. Never share your CNIC or NTN on unofficial websites.

Step 2: Select NTN Verification Option

  1. On the e-Services homepage, locate the verification section
  2. Click on "NTN Verification" or "Taxpayer Profile Inquiry"
  3. The system will redirect you to the verification form

Step 3: Enter Your Details

  1. CNIC Number: Enter your 13-digit CNIC without dashes (e.g., 3520112345678)
  2. NTN Number: Alternatively, you can enter your NTN if you know it
  3. Captcha Code: Enter the security code displayed on the screen

Step 4: Submit and View Results

  1. Click the "Verify" or "Search" button
  2. The system will process your request and display results within seconds
  3. Review your NTN verification details carefully

Information Displayed in Verification Results

Field Description
NTN Status Shows whether your NTN is Active or Inactive
Registered Name Your full name as registered with FBR
CNIC Number Your CNIC linked to the NTN
Registration Date When your NTN was first registered
Taxpayer Type Individual, AOP, or Company
ATL Status Active Taxpayers List inclusion status

Common Issues and Solutions

🛠️ Troubleshooting Online Verification

  • ❌ Portal not loading: Clear browser cache, try a different browser, or check your internet connection
  • ❌ "Invalid CNIC" error: Ensure you're entering all 13 digits without dashes or spaces
  • ❌ Captcha not displaying: Refresh the page or disable ad blockers temporarily
  • ❌ "Record not found": Your NTN may not be registered yet – see our NTN registration guide

📲 Method 2: Verify NTN via SMS

For quick NTN verification on mobile, you can use the FBR SMS service. This method is perfect when you need instant verification without internet access:

SMS Verification Steps

  1. Open your mobile messaging app
  2. Compose a new SMS
  3. Type: NTN (without brackets or dashes)
  4. Example: NTN 3520112345678
  5. Send the SMS to 9966
  6. Wait 1-2 minutes for the response message

📱 SMS Response Format

The reply SMS typically includes your NTN number, registered name, and current status. Standard SMS charges apply as per your mobile operator's rates. Keep the response for your records.

🔎 How to Check if NTN is Active or Inactive

Understanding your NTN status is crucial for tax compliance. Here's what different statuses mean and how to address them:

Active NTN Status

An Active NTN means:

  • ✅ You are registered in the FBR database
  • ✅ Your tax profile is current and up-to-date
  • ✅ You are (or can be) on the Active Taxpayers List (ATL)
  • ✅ You pay standard withholding tax rates
  • ✅ You can file tax returns and claim refunds

Inactive NTN Status

An Inactive NTN typically means:

  • ❌ You haven't filed tax returns for the required period
  • ❌ Your registration needs updating or renewal
  • ❌ You're not on the Active Taxpayers List
  • ❌ You pay higher withholding taxes (double rates)
  • ❌ Banking transactions may face complications

How to Reactivate Your NTN

  1. 1️⃣ Log in to the FBR IRIS portal
  2. 2️⃣ File any pending tax returns for previous years
  3. 3️⃣ Update your profile information if needed
  4. 4️⃣ Pay any outstanding taxes or penalties
  5. 5️⃣ Wait for the system to update (usually 24-48 hours)
  6. 6️⃣ Verify your status again using the methods above

🛠️ Common Problems During NTN Verification

NTN Not Found in System

If you receive a "Record Not Found" error during verification:

  • 🔍 Double-check your CNIC number for typos
  • 🔍 Verify you're using the correct format (13 digits, no dashes)
  • 🔍 Your NTN may not be registered yet
  • 🔍 Contact FBR helpline for system verification

If you haven't registered yet, follow our step-by-step NTN registration guide to get your NTN.

Name Mismatch Issues

Sometimes the name displayed in verification doesn't match your expectations:

  • 📝 FBR records may show your father's name along with yours
  • 📝 Name spelling differences from NADRA records
  • 📝 Married women's records may show maiden or husband's name

Solution: Visit your nearest FBR Regional Tax Office (RTO) with your original CNIC and request a name correction. This usually requires filling out a profile update form.

CNIC-Related Issues

Common CNIC problems during verification:

Issue Solution
CNIC not recognized Verify CNIC is valid and not expired. Update FBR records if you recently renewed your CNIC.
New CNIC not linked If you recently got a new CNIC, contact FBR to link it to your existing NTN.
Foreign CNIC holders Overseas Pakistanis should use POC (Pakistan Origin Card) or NICOP for verification.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How can I verify my NTN number online?

You can verify your NTN number online by visiting the FBR e-Services portal at e.fbr.gov.pk/esbn/, selecting "NTN Verification," entering your 13-digit CNIC without dashes, and clicking verify. Results display instantly showing your NTN status, registered name, and other details.

Is NTN verification free?

Yes, NTN verification is completely free through all official FBR channels. Online verification via the e-Services portal has no charges. SMS verification to 9966 only incurs standard SMS rates from your mobile operator. FBR helpline calls are charged at normal call rates.

What documents are needed for NTN verification?

For basic online verification, you only need your 13-digit CNIC number. No physical documents or uploads are required. However, if you need to correct information or resolve issues, you may need your original CNIC, proof of address, and access to your registered mobile number and email for verification codes.

How long does NTN verification take?

Online NTN verification through the FBR portal is instant – results appear within seconds of submitting your details. SMS verification typically returns results within 1-2 minutes. If contacting the FBR helpline, verification assistance may take 5-10 minutes depending on call volume.

Can I verify someone else's NTN?

Yes, you can verify any NTN or CNIC through the FBR e-Services portal. This is commonly done by businesses to verify vendors, by employers for new hires, or for property/vehicle transactions. The verification shows basic registration status and name but does not reveal sensitive financial information.

What is the difference between NTN and ATL status?

NTN is your permanent tax registration number. ATL (Active Taxpayers List) status indicates whether you have filed your most recent tax return. You can have an NTN but not be on the ATL if you haven't filed returns. ATL status is what determines lower withholding tax rates and other taxpayer benefits.
