# PakTaxCalc - Pakistan Tax Calculator Website

## Website Overview

PakTaxCalc (https://paktaxcalc.com) is a comprehensive, free online resource for calculating various taxes in Pakistan. The website provides accurate, up-to-date tax calculators and guides based on the latest FBR (Federal Board of Revenue) rates for 2025-2026.

## Main Features

### Tax Calculators
- **Salary Tax Calculator**: Calculate income tax on salaries based on current FBR tax slabs
- **PTA Mobile Import Tax Calculator**: Calculate custom duties on imported mobile phones
- **Business Tax Calculator**: Calculate taxes for businesses in Pakistan
- **Zakat Calculator**: Calculate Zakat amounts
- **Agricultural Tax Calculator**: Calculate agricultural income tax
- **Sales Tax Calculator**: Calculate general sales tax
- **Property Tax Calculator**: Calculate property taxes
- **Pension Calculator**: Calculate tax on pension income
