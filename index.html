Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common tax questions in Pakistan

What is the minimum income to pay tax in Pakistan?

Income up to Rs. 600,000 per year (Rs. 50,000/month) is completely tax-free for FY 2025-2026. If you earn less than this, you pay zero income tax.

How is salary tax calculated in Pakistan?

Tax is calculated using progressive slabs. Your annual income is divided into brackets (Rs. 0-600K, 600K-1.2M, etc.), and each bracket is taxed at its respective rate (0%, 1%, 11%, 23%, 30%, 35%, etc.). Higher income = higher tax rate.

What is PTA tax on mobile phones?

PTA charges 15-30% tax on imported phones based on value and registration type (CNIC/Passport). Tax is paid when registering the phone's IMEI within 60 days of import.

Is this calculator accurate?

Yes! We use official FBR tax rates for FY 2025-2026. Calculations are 100% accurate for standard cases. Updated regularly with latest government rates.

How do I file income tax return in Pakistan?

File online through FBR IRIS portal (iris.fbr.gov.pk). Login with your NTN, select tax year, fill income details, and submit. Deadline is September 30 every year. Read complete guide →

What is NTN and how do I get it?

NTN (National Tax Number) is your tax identification number. For individuals, it's the same as your CNIC. Register free online at FBR IRIS portal - takes 5-10 minutes. Step-by-step guide →

Do freelancers pay tax in Pakistan?

Yes. Freelancers earning from Fiverr, Upwork, or any platform must pay income tax. Same tax slabs apply. First Rs. 600,000 is tax-free. Banks deduct 1% withholding tax on foreign remittances. Complete freelancer guide →

What is the difference between filer and non-filer?

Filer: Person who files annual tax return (appears in Active Taxpayers List). Gets lower withholding tax rates on transactions.
Non-filer: Doesn't file return. Pays double tax on property, vehicles, and banking transactions. Always file to save money!

How much Zakat should I pay in Pakistan?

Zakat is 2.5% of your total wealth (savings, gold, investments) if it exceeds Nisab (Rs. 180,000 silver or Rs. 1,800,000 gold) for one lunar year. Complete Zakat guide →

What tax exemptions are available in Pakistan?

Tax-free allowances include: Medical allowance (10% of salary or Rs. 200K), House Rent (45% of basic), Conveyance, Leave encashment, Gratuity, Employer pension contributions. Complete exemptions list →

How to reduce salary tax legally?

Get medical allowance in salary structure, claim house rent allowance (if renting), invest in life insurance/pension funds (tax credits), maximize tax-free benefits, file annual return to claim refunds. 10 tax-saving tips →

What is capital gains tax on property in Pakistan?

Tax on profit when selling property: 0% if held 5+ years, 15% if less than 1 year, declining rates for 1-5 years. Non-filers pay double. Complete CGT guide →

Do I need to pay tax if my company already deducts it?

If your employer deducts correct tax monthly, you may not owe more. But you must still file annual return by Sep 30 to maintain filer status. File to claim refund if excess tax was deducted.

How long does FBR tax refund take?

FBR typically processes tax refunds in 45-90 days after filing. Refund is transferred directly to your bank account linked with IRIS portal. Track status online through FBR portal.

What happens if I don't file tax return?

Penalties: Late fee Rs. 1,000-40,000, become non-filer (double tax on transactions), bank account restrictions, cannot buy property/vehicle, legal prosecution in severe cases. Always file on time!

Can I deduct Zakat from income tax?

Yes! Zakat paid is 100% deductible from income tax in Pakistan. Provide receipts when filing return. You must pay both (Zakat is religious, tax is civic), but Zakat reduces your tax liability.

What is advance tax and who pays it?

Advance tax is paid quarterly throughout the year by business owners and self-employed persons. Helps spread tax burden instead of paying lump sum at year-end. Dates: March 15, June 15, Sept 15, Dec 15.

How to check filer status online?

Visit e.fbr.gov.pk/esbn/ → Select "Active Taxpayer List" → Enter CNIC → Search. Or SMS: ATL [CNIC] to 9966. If status shows "Active", you're a filer.

Do overseas Pakistanis pay tax in Pakistan?

Generally NO if you're non-resident (living abroad 183+ days/year) and income is earned abroad. But tax applies on Pakistan-sourced income (rental, business, property sale). Dual taxation treaties may apply.
