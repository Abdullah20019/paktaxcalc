‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum income to pay tax in Pakistan?

Income up to Rs. 600,000 per year (Rs. 50,000/month) is tax-free for FY 2025-2026.

How is salary tax calculated in Pakistan?

Tax is calculated using progressive slabs. Your annual income is divided into brackets, and each bracket is taxed at its respective rate.

What is PTA tax on mobile phones?

PTA charges 15-30% tax on imported phones based on value and registration type (CNIC/Passport).
