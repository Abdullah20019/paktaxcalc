How to Use the Estimate Properly

Start with the phone price in USD, then set the USD to PKR rate you want to use for comparison. Choose CNIC or passport registration depending on the route you expect to follow. The calculator converts the price into rupees and applies a rough tax estimate so you can see the likely total cost.
