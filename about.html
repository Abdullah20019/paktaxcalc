Our Mission

To make tax calculations accessible, accurate, and easy for every Pakistani. We believe financial literacy starts with understanding your taxes.

Whether you're a salaried employee, freelancer, business owner, or someone importing a mobile phone, PakTaxCalc provides instant, reliable tax calculations based on the latest FBR rates.

Who We Are

PakTaxCalc Team is a group of Pakistani developers familiar with the FBR tax system. We built this platform because we noticed how confusing and time-consuming tax calculations can be for ordinary Pakistanis.

After helping friends and family calculate their taxes manually countless times, we decided to create a simple, free tool that anyone can use. What started as a basic salary tax calculator in 2025 has grown into Pakistan's most comprehensive tax calculation platform.

Today, we serve thousands of users monthly - from salaried employees to freelancers, business owners to students calculating Zakat. Our goal remains the same: make tax math simple for everyone.
